Product Case Study

AI Agent & Job Simulation
Core Products & Go-to-market Strategy

Proposed core features and go-to-market strategy for an AI-powered job simulation.

#CaseStudy #ProductManagement #Research #Go-to-market

1. Business Task

Design a role-specific, AI-powered job simulation product that:

  • Enables learners to build & showcase practical skills
  • Provides credible proof of work (via AI agents)
  • Drives adoption with a clear GTM strategy

    • 2. My Role

    Product Manager/ Product Owner
