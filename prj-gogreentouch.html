Product Case Study

XanhSM GoGreen
Touchpoints Improvements

Redesigned user journey and optimized key app touchpoints to enhance engagement with the GoGreen feature.

#CaseStudy #ProductManagement #UX/UI

1. Business Task

Improve how GoGreen – XanhSM’s sustainability feature – is presented across key user touchpoints to:

  • Increase user trust through transparency
  • Make green contributions feel meaningful and seamless
  • Encourage ongoing engagement even outside of ride-booking moments

    • 2. My Role

    • Proposed features based on behavioral insights (nudges, gamification, social proof, personalization).
